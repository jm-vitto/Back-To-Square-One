House M.D. - Plot

Plot Overview

"House M.D." follows the unconventional and brilliant diagnostician Dr. Gregory House and his team of experts at Princeton-Plainsboro Teaching Hospital as they tackle complex medical cases. Dr. House, known for his acerbic wit and unorthodox methods, leads his team through a series of medical mysteries, often clashing with hospital administration and ethical dilemmas.

Each episode presents a new medical case, challenging Dr. House and his team to solve the diagnosis against the clock. The series delves into the personal lives of the characters, exploring their relationships, struggles, and ethical dilemmas faced in the high-stakes medical world.

With its compelling storytelling, engaging characters, and thought-provoking medical cases, "House M.D." captivated audiences worldwide and became one of the most beloved medical dramas in television history.
