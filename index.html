House M.D.

Overview

"House M.D." is a riveting medical drama series that follows the brilliant yet abrasive Dr. Gregory House and his team of diagnosticians at the Princeton-Plainsboro Teaching Hospital. With sharp wit and unorthodox methods, House tackles perplexing medical cases, often clashing with authorities. The series offers a blend of medical intrigue, character-driven storytelling, and ethical dilemmas.

Plot

The series revolves around Dr. House's unorthodox methods of diagnosing and treating patients with rare and unusual illnesses, featuring a new medical mystery each episode.
